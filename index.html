Institutional Overview

Established in Arghaun in 1992 A.D. and situated at Pokhara-5, Parshyang near Children Park at present, MAC imparts quality education in Science and Management streams in (+2) level, BScCSIT and BBS in Bachelor Level Offered by Tribhuvan University. The college has building with tranquil environment, spacious and airy classrooms,well maintained science labs with adequate appliances, Computer Lab, Electronics Lab,resourceful library, well managed cafeteria serving hygienic food,sports facilities,tutorial support classes,career counselling,leadership development programs,disciplined and cultured learning
